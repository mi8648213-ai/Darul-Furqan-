About Our School

DARUL-FURQAN TSANGAYA MODEL SCHOOL is committed to producing students who possess excellent Qur'anic knowledge, sound Islamic education, Arabic language proficiency, modern education, discipline, and good moral character.

Qur'an Memorization

Complete Hifz Programme with Tajweed.

Islamic Studies

Fiqh, Hadith, Tawheed, Tafsir and Seerah.

Arabic Language

Reading, Writing, Nahwu, Sarf and Balaghah.

Western Education

English, Mathematics, Computer and Sciences.
