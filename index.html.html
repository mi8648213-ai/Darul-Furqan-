OUR PROGRAMS

Programs and Sections

Our curriculum is organised into different levels and educational sections.

Primary / ابتدائي

Qur'an, Islamic Studies, Arabic Language and foundational education.

  • Qur'an memorization and revision
  • Islamic Studies
  • Arabic Language
  • English and Mathematics

Preparatory / إعدادي

A structured stage connecting primary studies with advanced Islamic and Arabic education.

  • Qur'an memorization and tasmi'
  • Tajwid and Qur'anic rules
  • Fiqh, Tawhid and Hadith
  • Arabic grammar and composition

Secondary / ثانوي

Advanced Qur'an, Islamic sciences, Arabic language and Western education.

  • Advanced Qur'an and Tajwid
  • Qira'at and Qur'anic sciences
  • Usul al-Fiqh and Logic
  • Arabic Literature and Balaghah

Boarding / القسم الداخلي

Full-day educational and tarbiyah programmes with structured daily schedules.

  • Daily memorization and revision
  • Qur'an writing and correction
  • Islamic studies
  • Daily worship and character development

Women Section

Islamic, Arabic, Western Education and entrepreneurship programmes.

  • English, Mathematics and Hausa
  • Islamic and Arabic studies
  • Entrepreneurship education
  • Practical skills development

Entrepreneurship

Practical skills designed to support self-reliance and livelihood development.

  • Liquid soap and dishwashing soap
  • Air freshener and hand wash
  • Germicide and hand sanitizer
  • Hair care and car wash products
